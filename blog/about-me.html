Who is AgileBelma?

Linguist

Started learning languages when I was 11 and started interpreting 'unofficially' when I was 12, so I kind of 'fell' into it. But I think it was mostly because I had wonderful language tutors throughout high school, who inspired me to be curious about many different languages and cultures. I'm still learning languages as it's my mission to do so until I physically can't.

Teacher and Researcher

I've wanted to be a teacher since I was 6 but became a professional academic about twenty years ago. I couldn't believe I was actually getting paid to teach and conduct research! Again, I was inspired by former teachers, lecturers and academics who showed me that if you have a passion for anything, you could actually pass it onto others. Learning is contagious.

Padawan Web Developer

I hold this role to be very similar to the first one as I'm learning a lot of syntax, semantics and lexical sets at the moment, which is my comfort zone. But it is still the syntax, semantics and lexical sets of another language, namely JavaScript, and this is the difficult part.

This website is a collection of my reflections as I travel on this winding road of becoming a web developer as a student of EDA. It may take a while so be patient with me. The semantic tags are almost in place but CSS is still work in progress as you can see. All feedback is welcome to improve my site, as long as you find me on Twitter @AgileBelma (I can't create web forms yet ;-)
