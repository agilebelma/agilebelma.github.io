What new things have you learnt about meditation?

Meditation is a state of being 'relaxed and alert at the same time', which is essential for web developers as they need to be flexible but firm in their approach to web design and development

When you're aware of your attention and meta-attention, you're focused and stable, calm and clear. The analogy I use to describe achieving this calm state is the snow-flake domes as tourist souvenirs. When you shake them, the 'snow flake' is like a storm inside, and slowly settles on the bottom, creating a snowy village picture. The mind works in a similar way in that it can get turbulent, and it needs to be grounded to process more information.

Meditation develops your muscle of attention to overcome resistance - just like working out in a gym (Chp 2).

The Process Model of Mindfulness Meditation

Intention > Attention > Cognitive Process > Attitude

Follow Breath > Distraction > Regain Attentional Focus

Distractions

  1. Acknowledge
  2. Experience without judging or reacting
  3. If you need to react, continue maintaining mindfulness
  4. Let it go
