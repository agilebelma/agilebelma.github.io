  3. Self-confidence

What is the relationship between mindfulness and self-awareness?

Mindfulness is type of strategy to pull yourself out of a tight situation, and self-awareness is knowing that you are in a tight situation as well as knowing all the resources that are available to you to get you out of that tight situation. Knowing your strengths and weaknesses

Both mindfulness and self-awareness are 'trainable' qualities

Body Scan

Starting from the top, going all the way to the tip of the toes, bringing attention to your whole body without any judgment

This takes time getting used to but I use an app to talk me through it. I actually tried this technique at the airport, after my flight was cancelled. Whilst I was waiting, I did a body scan in about 5 mins. I had to wait at the queue to transfer the flight to another day for over 2 hours! So the 5 mins I spent on body scan was the best investment ever!

Journalling

I love journalling! Things have a habit of going around in circles in your head! Well, in my head anyway. So, when I finally get them off my chest, I feel great, really do. Journalling helps me re-assess my situation and re-ground emotions just like doing a body scan.

"My emotions are Not Me"

Sometimes my emotions feel like a snow globe, like the tourist souvenir that you shake and it looks like snowing on the inside. I need a moment or two to settle that snow storm inside me. The feeling can be over-powering, almost nauseating, and that is not me, but how I'm made to feel physically. It feels frustrating because I cannot control it, especially when it is imposed.
