Connecting

"If we treat people with kindness and empathy, we create trust and understanding" Chade-Meng Tan
Have you tried using any of the insight and techniques for growing trust and sincerity? How did it go?

Yes! I was absolutely furious with a colleague, so much so I almost avoided going to the team meeting, in order to avoid the colleague. But, few minutes into the meeting [it was one of those meetings that required a break half way through], I decided get a cup of coffee. On the way back, I passed my colleague, and decided to make a peace offering, by offering a cup of coffee to the same colleague. It somehow felt good to get rid of my 'Darth Vader' thoughts and use the force for good. It just took a cup of coffee to feel the force!

A new colleague had to go away to another town for the funeral of a family member. When he came back, I had a chance to ask him how he was. I thought it was appropriate despite not knowing him well. I asked with kindness. He was surprised but seemed happy to be asked. I then asked him about the family member who passed away. We sat down in the lunch room and talked about his uncle for about 20 mins or so. I got to meet the new staff member and found out about his uncle too. It wasn't easy to get started but he appreciated the time and attention. I think I would too.

Political Awaraness

"If you understand people and you understand the interactions between them, you will understand the whole organization."

Practices for Political Awareness
  1. Maintain rich personal networks.
  2. Practice reading the underlying currents of your organization.
  3. Distinguish between your self-interest, the interest of your team, and the organization's interest.
  4. Utilize your self-awareness to better understand your role in the web of personalities and interactions.
